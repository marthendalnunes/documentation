title: "Introduction"
description: "Utility integrations are designed to streamline the development process, offering practical tools and utilities for your app, or to assist in creating new integrations."
---

Utility integrations are meant to provide utils for either Web3 problems such as creating short-lived wallets, or for core TurboETH development, such as creating a new integration. Currently, there are two utility integrations available:

- [🗝️ Session Keys](/integration/utility/session-keys)
- [🏁 Starter](/integration/utility/starter)
